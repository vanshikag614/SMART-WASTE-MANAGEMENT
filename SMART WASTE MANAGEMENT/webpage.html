    WHO ARE WE AND WHAT DO WE DO?

    India generates nearly 62 million tonnes of garbage each year and it is mainly by 377 million people living in urban
    India,now it is the world's 3rd largest garbage generator however nearly 31 million tonnes of waste are not treated and
    dumped in landfill sites.The increasing larger quantities of solid waste are unable to be managed effectively.
    This problem can be solved by the active interaction of public regarding this. Our project enhances the interaction
    and also creates awareness among the efficient management of solid waste. Here our website becomes an interface
    for the collection of solid waste and converting it from waste to energy form or recycling it in its own ways.


    Two kinds of activities could take place here,
    1.Customers can seperate the waste based on their recycling type and can sell their wastes for a fixed amount
    depending on the type of waste.
    2.Customers can buy the waste so that they can recycle it or convert it from waste to energy form. We aim
    to reduce and recycle wastes in order to protect the environment by lessening the need to extract resources,
    to lower the potential for contamination and also to reduce expenses for disposal.
